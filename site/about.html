About the Tournament

Since its first edition

A gathering, not a competition.

The tournament represents an esteemed gathering uniting dignitaries and golf enthusiasts globally, offering networking opportunities and friendly competition in spectacular surroundings. The event serves as an ideal venue for demonstrating golfing ability while connecting with prominent individuals across diverse fields.

Now in its fifth edition, the Head of State Invitational continues to bring together heads of state, diplomats, business leaders and golf enthusiasts for a day built around fair play, good company, and the shared discipline of the game.

About Course

Achimota Golf Club

Set within the historic Achimota Forest Reserve in Accra, Achimota Golf Club is one of Ghana's most storied fairways — a course where towering hardwoods frame every hole and the pace of play matches the calm of the forest around it. Its greens have welcomed generations of golfers, from weekend regulars to visiting heads of state, and remain the home of the Head of State Invitational every year.
